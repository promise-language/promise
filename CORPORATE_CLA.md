within the scope of their employment, they may sign only the Individual CLA.
When in doubt, sign both.

**"Corporation"** means the legal entity that signs this Agreement and is
identified in the Signature section below.

**"Contribution"** means any original work of authorship, including any
modifications or additions to an existing work, that your employees
intentionally submit to Promise Lang for inclusion in, or documentation of,
any of the products owned or managed by Promise Lang LLC. For purposes of this
definition, "submit" means any form of electronic, verbal, or written
communication sent to Promise Lang, including but not limited to communication
via GitHub pull requests, issues, code review tools, mailing lists, and chat
systems.

**"Authorized Employee"** means any current or future employee of the
Corporation who is authorized by the Corporation to make Contributions on its
behalf. The Corporation may maintain a list of Authorized Employees by notifying
Promise Lang LLC in writing.
